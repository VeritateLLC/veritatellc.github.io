About Veritate

Veritate LLC develops digital resources designed to help Catholics understand, prepare for, and practice the faith with greater confidence.

Our work seeks to preserve Catholic tradition while using modern technology responsibly and respectfully.

Our App

Veritate: Confessio

Veritate: Confessio helps Catholics prepare for the Sacrament of Reconciliation through guided examinations of conscience, confession preparation, traditional and modern confession formats, prayers, and spiritual guidance.

The app supports preparation for Confession. It does not replace a priest, sacramental confession, absolution, or pastoral care.
