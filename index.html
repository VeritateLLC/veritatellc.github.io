Technology in Service of Truth

Veritate LLC develops practical digital resources that support Catholic prayer, sacramental preparation, and faithful living.

Our purpose

Faithful by Design

Our work uses modern technology without treating the faith as modern content. Each product aims for clarity, reverence, and usefulness.

Technology can assist preparation and learning. It cannot replace the Church, a priest, the sacraments, or sound pastoral judgment.

Our first application

Veritate: Confessio

Confessio helps Catholics prepare for the Sacrament of Reconciliation through examinations of conscience, prayers, confession guidance, and traditional resources.

The app supports preparation for confession. It does not hear confessions, administer a sacrament, grant absolution, or replace a Catholic priest.
