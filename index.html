Veritate: Confessio

Veritate: Confessio is a Catholic confession preparation application. It helps users examine their conscience, prepare for the Sacrament of Penance, review common prayers, and record an assigned penance.

The application includes guidance for both the traditional and modern forms commonly encountered in Catholic confession.

Veritate: Confessio does not replace the counsel of a priest or the Sacrament of Confession.
